TIPS AND TRICKS - FISH TANK

1. To lower pH, use natural driftwood or peat moss. To raise pH, add crushed coral to your filter.

2. Always test water pH before adding it to your tank. Consider reverse osmosis or deionized water to maintain the desired pH balance.

TIPS AND TRICKS - GROW BED

1. To improve soil pH in low-pH locations, mix finely ground limestone into the soil before planting or fertilize with calcium nitrate as recommended by soil tests.

2. Mix 2-4 inches of compost or well-rotted manure into the top 6-8 inches of soil to buffer pH fluctuations and provide nutrients.

TIPS AND TRICKS - FISH TANK TEMPERATURE

1. Check if the sensor is properly connected, and no wires are disconnected.

2. Set your thermostat to the required temperature.

TIPS AND TRICKS - GROW BED TEMPERATURE

1. Fill the box with manure and bedding, water and turn well. If your manure/bedding mixture does not heat up well the first time even with plenty of water and turning, you may have too much bedding.

2. Cover the box with greenhouse plastic or row cover to trap heat at night.
